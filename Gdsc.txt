Hello I want to apply for gdsc lead of my college, and I'm required to make a two minute video about me. Can you recommend what I should say?

Certainly, I'd be happy to help you out! Here are some general guidelines and ideas you can consider when making your two-minute video:

Introduce yourself: Start by introducing yourself, your name, and the course you are studying.

Explain why you want to be a GDSC Lead: Talk about your motivation for applying for the position and what attracted you to the role.

Share your relevant experience: If you have any relevant experience in leading teams, organizing events or technical projects, or volunteering, mention them briefly and explain how they have prepared you for the role of a GDSC Lead.

Showcase your skills: Highlight your strengths, such as your communication skills, leadership abilities, technical expertise, or creativity, and explain how these skills will help you in your role as a GDSC Lead.

Describe your vision: Share your ideas and vision for the GDSC in your college. Explain what you want to achieve as a GDSC Lead and how you plan to engage students and build a community around technology.

Conclude with a call to action: End your video with a strong call to action, encouraging students to get involved with the GDSC and expressing your enthusiasm for the opportunity to serve as their GDSC Lead.

Remember, your video is a chance to showcase your personality, passion, and skills, so don't be afraid to inject your own unique style and energy into your presentation. Good luck with your application!
